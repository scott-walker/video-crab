What it does

Drop a video.
Get everything out of it.

01 Transcribe Whisper-powered speech-to-text. Segment by region, navigate by timestamp, multi-language.
02 Analyze 3-stage context-aware AI pipeline. Detailed narrative, topics, key moments. Ask about specific aspects with voice.
03 Trim & Clip Waveform timeline, drag-to-select, AI-suggested clip boundaries. Export with FFmpeg.
04 Listen TTS narration of analyses. Section-based streaming, built-in player, per-session caching.
05 Organize Projects with auto-save. All transcripts and analyses persist. Pick up where you left off.

How it works

From raw video to
structured insights

The 3-stage analysis pipeline ensures accurate understanding of your content, even for multi-hour recordings.

01

Context Extraction

AI reads the full transcript to understand participants, topic, and narrative arc.

02

Parallel Analysis

10-minute chunks analyzed in parallel, each with full video context for accuracy.

03

Synthesis

Chunk analyses merged into one cohesive narrative with topics, moments, and clips.
